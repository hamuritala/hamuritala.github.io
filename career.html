Software engineer





Software engineers are computer science professionals who use knowledge of engineering principles and programming languages to build software products, develop computer games, and run network control systems. The average salary for a software engineer in the United States is around $92,824 per year. Some skills needed for a software engineer are Software development, Object-oriented design and much more. Software engineers usually need a bachelor's degree in computer science, software engineering or a related technical field. Some employers may require that candidates hold a master's degree. Business analyst





A business analyst is a person who analyzes an organization or business domain and documents its business, processes, or systems, assessing the business model or its integration with technology. The average Business Analyst salary in the United States is $76,729 as of December 28, 2020, but the salary range typically falls between $68,901 and $85,719. Some skills needed for a business analyst are interpersonal and consultative skills, analytical thinking/problem solving and knowledge of business structure. Business analyst typically have a bachelor's degree in business or related field or an MBA. They have a minimum of 5 years experience in business analysis or a related field.
